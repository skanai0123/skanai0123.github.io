A compact reference page for several optimization problems in both QUBO and Ising form.

Variables: \(x_i \in \{0,1\},\; s_i \in \{-1,1\}\) Conversion: \(x_i = \frac{1-s_i}{2}\) Problems: Max-Cut / Max-SAT

For Max-SAT, the general form becomes higher-order. The page shows the structure and keeps the formulas short enough to scan.
